Malai Sandwich

Description

Malai Sandwich is a sweet delight featuring a luscious filling of cardamom-infused malai between soft bread layers. Garnished with pistachios and almonds, it's a perfect blend of creamy richness and nutty crunch.

Simplicity meets decadence in the Malai Sandwich, making it a favored choice for celebrations or a sweet indulgence anytime. This unique dessert offers a delightful twist to the conventional sandwich, bringing a touch of sweetness to every bite.

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices: Typically white bread or milk bread, as needed.
  • Malai (Clotted Cream): About 1 cup.
  • Sugar: 1/4 to 1/2 cup, as per taste.
  • Cardamom Powder: 1/2 teaspoon, for flavor.
  • Chopped Nuts: Almonds, Pistachios (about 2 tablespoons), for garnish.
  • Saffron Strands: A pinch, optional, for added fragrance and color.
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter): 1 tablespoon, for greasing and added richness.

Steps

  1. Prepare Malai Filling: In a bowl, mix 1 cup of malai (clotted cream) with 1/4 to 1/2 cup of sugar (adjust to taste) and 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder. Combine well to form a sweet and creamy filling.
  2. Optional: Add Saffron: For additional fragrance and color, you can add a pinch of saffron strands to the malai mixture. Stir to incorporate.
  3. Chop Nuts: Finely chop about 2 tablespoons of almonds and pistachios. These will be used for garnishing the sandwich.
  4. Assemble the Sandwich: Take two slices of bread and spread a generous layer of the malai mixture on one slice. Place the second slice on top, creating a sandwich.
  5. Garnish with Chopped Nuts: Sprinkle the finely chopped nuts on top of the malai layer. Press down slightly so that the nuts adhere to the malai.
  6. Optional: Grease with Ghee: If desired, lightly brush the outer sides of the sandwich with ghee for added richness.
  7. Slice and Serve: Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters. Serve immediately and enjoy this delightful Malai Sandwich as a sweet treat.
